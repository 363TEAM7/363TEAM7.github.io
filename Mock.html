Introduction

In our design, we developed Mockups using mockfkow.com,we considered navigation through the webpages and shown the sequence of actions on each of the images of the prototype to show how to reach a specific page.

In addition, prototypes were developed assuming that the majority of screens are based on an aspect ratio of 16:9. We tried to keep the pages as clear as possible by maximizing the white space, this will provide us with an easy way of optimizing the pages to small devices screen sizes without losing any items from any page.

As for the usability of the online library system, the user will be shown a homepage upon his first visit and he can sign in, if he doesn’t have an account he’ll have to visit the library and provide his details to the library assistant in order to register.After signing in if the user is a student he’ll be redirected to the homepage to search for a book, and if he is a staff member he will be redirected to the library online portal.
